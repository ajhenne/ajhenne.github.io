XRT Lightcurve

The burst appears to have two flares early in the lightcurve, at 64.4—82.9s and 95.9—161s. The best fit includes a powerlaw of 5 breaks.

Figure 1: Swift XRT lightcurve. Flux against the trigger time. For this burst, T0=632108250.112s.

Fitted Lightcurve (lcfit)
